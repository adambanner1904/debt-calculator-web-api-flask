Debt Calculator

This page is to explain how to use this tool. As you can see from the top of this page, it is a form of debt calculator, more specifically you input certain things like your salary and the amount that your debt is and so on and this program will tell you how long it will take to pay off that debt.

When you press next you will taken to a page where it will ask you for some information like your salary and amount that you can put into paying off loans. When this is all done, we will break down how long it will take you to pay off the debts and how much you might save by increasing the amount that you pay back.
